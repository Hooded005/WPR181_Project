Accommodation

Accommodation will be available to the first 30 guests that RSVP (30 rooms with 2 beds per room). So RSVP quick so you can guarantee that you have a place to stay for the night.

The accommodation wil be at the Sun City Forever Resort itself. The accommodation price for the night is R450 p.p. This will only cover for the night of the wedding. After the night, accommodation prices return to normal. To book your accommodation just tick the accommodation option on the RSVP page
